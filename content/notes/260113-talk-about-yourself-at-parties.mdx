***Tell good stories***

How do you introduce yourself to new people? Be honest and humble while explaining what it is that you do, they're not interroagating you, treat them as an oppotunity to connect with people. 
<Callout type="quote">
  Tell the truth and tell it with dignity and self-respect.
</Callout>
Those principles apply to your bio as well, keep it short and sweet, don't get cute, don't brag. You're a photographer, not an "amazing", "aspiring", "creative" photographer, just a photographer.

Also,
<Callout type="quote">
  Unless you are actually a ninja, a guru, or a rock star, don't ever use any of those terms in your bio. Ever.
</Callout>
