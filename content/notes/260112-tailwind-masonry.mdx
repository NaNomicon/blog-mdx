Tailwind's `columns-` utilities are great for simple masonry.

```html
<div class="columns-1 md:columns-2 lg:columns-3 gap-4">
  <div class="mb-4 break-inside-avoid">...</div>
</div>
```

The `break-inside-avoid` is crucial to prevent cards from splitting between columns.
